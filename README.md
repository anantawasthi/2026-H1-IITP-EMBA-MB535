## ğŸ“š Course Overview

This course explores the evolution of HR from intuition-based to analytical thinking, focusing on frameworks and metrics that drive organizational value.

### [Curriculum](MB 535 HR Analytics) Highlights

- **Unit I**: Evolution of HR Analytics, HRIS data sources, and frameworks (LAMP, HR Scorecard).

- **Unit II**: Quantitative dimensions of HR planning and recruitment analytics (bias detection, turnover prediction).

- **Unit III**: Performance analysis, training effectiveness, and compensation analytics.

- **Unit IV**: Stress level tracking, mediation processes, and evidence-based practices.

- **Unit V**: Applications of HR Metrics and creating HR Dashboards.
